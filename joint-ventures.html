FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How does the 50/50 split work?

You receive 50% of the assignment fee, we receive 50% for disposing the deal. This ensures everyone is motivated to close the deal quickly and profitably.

How fast can my deal close?

Our network consists of serious, pre-qualified investors with ready capital. Most deals are matched with buyers within 24-48 hours and can close within 7-14 days, depending on the property and location.

What types of deals do you accept?

We work with all types of wholesale deals including single-family homes, multi-family properties, land, and commercial real estate. As long as there's a solid spread and the numbers make sense, we can help.

Do I need to have the property under contract?

Yes, you should have a signed purchase agreement with the seller before submitting. This ensures we can move quickly to connect you with buyers and close the deal.
